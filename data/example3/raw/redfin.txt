Experience tranquility, style, and comfort in this sun-drenched, waterfront located, two-story end unit. Located on a spacious 4,256 sq ft corner lot, it offers privacy and convenience. Step out to a deck and private dock, perfect for direct water access. Inside, discover fresh paint, gleaming wide-plank wood floors, beamed ceilings, and recessed lighting. Abundant natural light pours in through large windows, glass doors, and skylights. The layout features a formal dining room and a step-down living room with a cozy fireplace and high ceilings, creating a warm ambiance. The inviting kitchen boasts an eating area, stainless appliances, granite counters, and access to a vibrant outdoor patio. A sizable ground-floor bedroom offers a peaceful retreat with a stone fireplace, custom built-ins, and a wall of windows. The open-air staircase is a true architectural marvel, enhancing the home's sense of space and comfort. Upstairs, two large en-suite bedrooms provide private sanctuaries with high angled ceilings, updated baths, and ample closets. Practical amenities include an oversized two-car garage with storage and a separate laundry/mud room. Enjoy a prime location near top-rated schools, parks, trails, library, transit, shopping, community pool, hot tub and tennis courts.
